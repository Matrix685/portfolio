History

This website is a recreation of Google Site's "Club" default layout with a few of my own design choices added in. The hover effect on the "Events" page was my own creation and there were a few things I didn't want to add because I didn't know what to put in them or I didn't think it would be worth it to recreate them.

Dedication is important

Why we did this

I decided to recreate Google's site because I really liked it's design. Looking through their features I thought some of them were really cool, and that they would be fun to recreate. There were a couple of things that I thought were missing though, and this was another reason why I wanted to remake the site from scratch.
